DEPOSITION OF HAROLD WINSTED
Case No.: CV-2024-08891
Pinnacle Tech Solutions, Inc. v. Quantum Dynamics LLC et al.

Date: April 10, 2024
Location: Specter & Litt LLP, 100 Park Avenue, New York, NY 10017

Appearances:
  For Plaintiff: Harvey Specter, Esq., Specter & Litt LLP
  For Defendant: Dana Reyes, Esq., Reyes & Morrison LLP
  Court Reporter: Angela Brooks, CSR

---

EXAMINATION BY MR. SPECTER:

Q: Please state your full name for the record.
A: Harold James Winsted.

Q: And your current title?
A: Managing Director of Quantum Dynamics LLC.

Q: Mr. Winsted, prior to signing the Agreement with Pinnacle on September 1, 2023,
did you make representations to Jennifer Park regarding Quantum's past experience?

A: I may have mentioned some prior work. Yes.

Q: I'm going to show you what has been marked as Plaintiff's Exhibit 7 — an email
from you dated August 22, 2023. Would you read the highlighted portion?

A: It says, "Quantum has successfully deployed identical enterprise data pipeline
systems for multiple Fortune 500 clients over the past two years."

Q: Did you write that?
A: It appears so, yes.

Q: Was that statement true?
A: We had worked on similar projects.

Q: That's not what I asked. Had Quantum ever delivered a fully operational
enterprise data pipeline system, of the kind described in the Agreement, to any
client?

A: [Pause] Not — not in its entirety, no.

Q: So the statement was false?
A: It was aspirational. We had partial deployments.

Q: Were you paid Milestone 1 — $950,000 — on or around October 1, 2023?
A: Yes.

Q: And Milestone 2 — another $950,000 — on December 1, 2023?
A: Yes.

Q: As of today, April 10, 2024, has Quantum delivered any version of the System?
A: We have delivered design documents and—

Q: A functioning system. Yes or no?
A: No.

Q: And you received $1,900,000 in total from Pinnacle?
A: Correct.

Q: What happened to those funds?
A: They were used for operating expenses. Salaries. Infrastructure costs.

Q: Did Quantum maintain any escrow or hold-back of client funds in case of
project failure?
A: No.

Q: After the January 31, 2024 delivery deadline passed, did Quantum communicate
with Pinnacle?
A: We sent an internal update in early February.

Q: To Pinnacle?
A: I — I believe counsel advised us not to communicate directly after that point.

MR. SPECTER: Let the record reflect the witness paused for approximately
ten seconds before answering.

Q: Did Quantum ever intend to hire sufficient engineering staff to complete
the System?
A: We intended to. The market for engineers was very competitive.

Q: Quantum had three engineers on staff at project inception, correct?
A: Yes, approximately.

Q: The project specification called for a minimum team of twelve?
A: That was an aspirational figure in the Schedule.

Q: I have no further questions at this time.

---

CROSS-EXAMINATION BY MS. REYES:

Q: Mr. Winsted, you testified that Quantum used milestone payments for
operating expenses. Were those expenses related to the Pinnacle project?
A: Substantially yes. We were ramping up for the engagement.

Q: Did Quantum make good-faith efforts to recruit additional engineers?
A: Yes, we posted positions and conducted interviews throughout Q4 2023.

Q: Thank you. Nothing further.
