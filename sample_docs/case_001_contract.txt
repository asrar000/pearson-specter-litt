SOFTWARE SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Software Services Agreement (this "Agreement") is entered into as of
September 1, 2023 (the "Effective Date") by and between:

  PINNACLE TECH SOLUTIONS, INC., a Delaware corporation ("Client"), and
  QUANTUM DYNAMICS LLC, a New York limited liability company ("Vendor").

---

1. SERVICES

1.1 Vendor shall design, develop, test, and deliver a custom enterprise data
pipeline system (the "System") as described in Schedule A attached hereto.

1.2 The System shall be capable of processing a minimum of 10,000 records per
hour with 99.9% uptime measured on a monthly rolling basis.

1.3 The System shall be delivered in fully operational condition no later than
January 31, 2024 (the "Delivery Deadline").

---

2. FEES AND PAYMENT SCHEDULE

2.1 Total Contract Value. The total fee for the Services is TWO MILLION EIGHT
HUNDRED FIFTY THOUSAND U.S. DOLLARS ($2,850,000).

2.2 Milestone Payments:
  Milestone 1 — Project kickoff (due October 1, 2023): $950,000
  Milestone 2 — Beta delivery (due December 1, 2023): $950,000
  Milestone 3 — Final delivery and acceptance (due February 15, 2024): $950,000

2.3 Invoices are due net-15 from receipt. Late payments accrue interest at 1.5%
per month.

---

3. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

3.1 Vendor warrants that: (a) it has the expertise and resources to deliver the
System; (b) the System will conform to the specifications in Schedule A; and
(c) all deliverables will be original work or properly licensed.

3.2 Vendor represents that it has successfully delivered comparable data pipeline
systems to enterprise clients in the past 24 months.

---

4. DEFAULT AND REMEDIES

4.1 An "Event of Default" shall occur if Vendor fails to deliver the System by
the Delivery Deadline and does not cure such failure within fifteen (15) calendar
days of written notice from Client.

4.2 Upon an Event of Default, Client may, at its election: (a) terminate this
Agreement; (b) demand return of all milestone payments previously made; and
(c) seek any additional damages permitted by law.

---

5. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

5.1 Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or
punitive damages arising from this Agreement, except in cases of fraud,
intentional misconduct, or gross negligence.

---

6. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION

6.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York without
regard to conflicts of law principles.

6.2 Any dispute arising under this Agreement shall be resolved in the state or
federal courts located in New York County, New York. Both parties consent to
personal jurisdiction therein.

---

7. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

7.1 This Agreement, including all Schedules, constitutes the entire agreement of
the parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior
negotiations and representations.
