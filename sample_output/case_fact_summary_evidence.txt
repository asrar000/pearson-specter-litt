Pinnacle Tech Solutions, Inc. and Quantum Dynamics LLC entered into a Software Services Agreement on September 1, 2023. Pinnacle is the plaintiff and Quantum Dynamics LLC is the defendant. The agreement required Quantum to build a custom enterprise data pipeline system by January 31, 2024. Pinnacle paid the first milestone payment of $950,000 on October 1, 2023, and the second milestone payment of $950,000 on December 1, 2023. The contract required the system to process at least 10,000 records per hour and maintain 99.9% uptime. Quantum was to provide design documentation, architecture diagrams, and an installation plan. Pinnacle issued written delivery demands on February 5, 2024 and February 22, 2024. Quantum failed to deliver a functioning system by the deadline and provided only an unfinished prototype. The complaint alleges breach of contract, fraudulent misrepresentation, and unjust enrichment. The plaintiff claims damages of $2,850,000 plus attorney fees. The parties are citizens of New York and Delaware, and the case is filed in the Superior Court of New York County.
